Privacy Policy for DropTV

Last Updated: December 2, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to DropTV, a non-profit live streaming channel for arts and sports. We care about your privacy and do not collect or store any personal information from our viewers.

Data Collection

DropTV does not collect any personal data. We do not track your activity or use cookies.

Usage of the Channel

DropTV is a non-profit channel, and we do not engage in commercial activities or collect any data for business purposes.

Security

We take security seriously and ensure that our platform remains safe for all viewers, even though we do not collect any personal information.

Third-Party Services

We do not share any data with third parties. There are no advertisements or tracking from external companies on DropTV.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will post the changes on this page with the updated date.
